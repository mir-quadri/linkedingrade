§ 02 — METHOD

Why a Chrome extension, and not "just ask ChatGPT."

A general-purpose LLM is a brilliant intern with no glasses. It can't see the page, can't compare you to a population, and can't reproduce its own answer twice. We built the audit around the three things ChatGPT can't do.

01 / VISIBILITY

It reads the live page. The actual one.

Activity history, endorsement freshness, banner-image composition, posting cadence, mutual-connection signal — none of which you can paste into a chat window without flattening it to text and losing the signal.

02 / RUBRIC

Six sections, 38 weighted signals, calibrated against 38,412 audited profiles. Re-run the same profile a year from now and you'll get a grade that's directly comparable. Ask GPT twice and you'll get two different answers and two different vibes.
